Welcome to Zedos. By registering as a seller on the Zedos app you agree to the following terms and conditions. Please read carefully before listing or selling any products.

1. Eligibility

  • Sellers may be individuals or registered businesses operating in Zambia.
  • Sellers must be at least 18 years old.
  • All registration information must be true, accurate, and up-to-date.

2. Seller Responsibilities

  • Sellers manage their own store, listings, stock levels, and buyer communication.
  • Product descriptions, images and prices must be accurate and not misleading.
  • Sale of counterfeit, illegal, expired, or restricted items is strictly prohibited.
  • Repeated failure to respond to orders or messages may be considered abandonment and your store may be flagged as inactive or fraudulent.

3. Account Activity

  • Accounts must be logged into at least once every six (6) months to remain active.
  • Accounts inactive for more than 6 months may be automatically closed or archived.
  • Abandoned or repeatedly inactive stores may be permanently removed.

4. Transactions and Payments

  • Currently, transactions are handled directly between buyer and seller.
  • Zedos does not process or guarantee payments at this stage.
  • Zedos may introduce in-app payment options (e.g., MoMo, Airtel Money) in the future.
  • Sellers must provide accurate payment details to buyers.

5. Order Fulfilment and Cancellations

  • Sellers are responsible for preparing and delivering all confirmed orders.
  • Orders should be updated to the correct status (e.g., Preparing, In-Transit, Delivered).
  • Zedos may automatically close an order if it remains unanswered or stuck under Preparing/In-Transit for more than 7 days from the purchase date.
  • Each order cancellation results in a –30 points deduction from the seller's performance score. Repeated cancellations may lead to account suspension.

6. Deliveries

  • Sellers arrange their own deliveries or pickups and are responsible for ensuring items arrive in good condition and on time.
  • Zedos is not responsible for lost, damaged, or delayed deliveries.

7. Disputes and Complaints

  • Buyers and sellers must attempt to resolve disputes directly.
  • If no solution is found, Zedos may intervene and take appropriate action, including warnings or suspensions.
  • Zedos does not guarantee refunds or replacements on behalf of sellers.

8. Listing Policy

  • Listing products and services on Zedos is completely free.
  • Listings must comply with Zambian law and Zedos community guidelines.
  • Zedos reserves the right to remove listings that violate these terms without prior notice.

9. Fees and Commissions

  • Zedos currently charges no listing fees or commissions. Future fee changes will be communicated in advance.
