Agent Terms & Conditions

These terms are designed to protect genuine agents, tenants, and landlords, and to promote transparency and trust in the rental market.

1. Agent Verification & Landlord Relationship

Every agent must undergo a verification process before being allowed to list properties.

  • You must be able to prove that you are well known, trusted, and authorized by the landlord.
  • This may include landlord confirmation via phone, in-app approval, or documentation.
  • Any listing without a clear landlord–agent link may be rejected or removed.

2. Service Fee Policy (No Viewing Fees)

Viewing fees are strictly prohibited.
  • A service (success) fee is payable only when the tenant decides to occupy the house.
  • No payment must be requested before the client has made a decision.
  • The service fee must be declared upfront and agreed upon once.
  • No extra charges beyond the agreed amount are allowed.
  • If a tenant chooses to appreciate your service further, it must be voluntary and never requested.

3. Accurate & Honest Property Listings

We understand that moving around and showing houses for free can be frustrating. To reduce unnecessary movements and protect your time:

  • Upload clear and recent photos of the entire house.
  • Include all rooms, toilets, kitchen, surroundings, and overall exterior.
  • Clearly show any faults, damages, or issues.
  • All images must reflect the actual house in its current condition.
Fake, misleading, edited, or unrelated photos are strictly prohibited.

4. Payment Conduct & Professional Ethics

  • Agents must behave professionally, respectfully, and honestly.
  • No pressure, misrepresentation, or fee manipulation is allowed.
  • Kindness matters — how you treat your first client determines your next one.

5. Ratings & Client Feedback

  • Clients can rate agents based on honesty, transparency, and overall experience.
  • Poor ratings and repeated complaints affect visibility and trust status.
  • Continued violations may lead to suspension or removal.

6. Reporting & Enforcement

  • Clients may report fake agents or violations at any time.
  • Once reported, your listings will be hidden immediately.
  • You will be contacted to respond or clarify.
  • If we do not hear from you within 3 days, further action will be taken.
Failure to respond may result in permanent deletion of your account and all listings.

7. Agent Discussion Forum

  • The app provides a discussion forum for agents.
  • This space is used to discuss fees, best practices, and policies.
  • Encouraging scams, hidden fees, or policy violations is not allowed.

8. Final Agreement

By continuing to use this platform as an agent, you confirm that you are genuine, understand these terms, and agree to operate transparently and fairly.

Our goal is simple: protect genuine agents, protect tenants, and clean up the rental market together.
