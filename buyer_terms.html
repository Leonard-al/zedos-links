4. Payments Updated

💳 Mobile money payments are now live on Zedos. We strongly encourage all buyers to pay through the Zedos in-app payment system for a safer, more protected shopping experience. In-app payments include buyer protection — your money is held securely until you confirm you have received your order.

  • Payments on Zedos are made via mobile money through the in-app payment system. Buyers are strongly encouraged to use this method for all transactions.
  • Escrow protection: When you pay in-app, your payment is temporarily held by Zedos and is not released to the seller until you confirm that you have received your order in good condition.
  • Once you confirm receipt of your order, the held funds are released to the seller promptly.
  • If you do not confirm receipt within 48 hours of the recorded delivery, Zedos may prompt you to confirm or raise a dispute. Failure to respond may result in the funds being automatically released to the seller.
  • Buyers should never pay outside the Zedos platform. Payments made directly to sellers (e.g., cash or external transfers) are not covered by Zedos buyer protection and are made entirely at the buyer's own risk.
  • Always keep in-app payment receipts and confirmation records for your reference.

  • Cancelling an order for any reason results in -30 points.

6. Cancellations and Refunds

  • If you paid via the Zedos in-app payment system and your order is cancelled before delivery, your held payment will be refunded to your original payment method.
  • If an item arrives damaged, incorrect, or not as described, raise a dispute within 48 hours of delivery confirmation. Zedos will review the case and the held payment may be refunded at Zedos' discretion.
  • Cancellations and refunds for orders paid outside the platform are handled directly between buyer and seller; Zedos does not guarantee such refunds but may assist in unresolved disputes.

7. Disputes and Complaints
