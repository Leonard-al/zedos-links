01

Agent Verification & Landlord Relationship

Every agent must complete a verification process before listing any property on LandNant. This safeguards tenants and upholds the credibility of every listing on the platform.

  • You must demonstrate that you are recognised, trusted, and explicitly authorised by the landlord to act on their behalf.
  • Authorisation may be verified via landlord confirmation call, in-app approval, or supporting documentation.
  • Any listing without a verifiable landlord–agent link will be rejected or removed without notice.
  • Impersonating a landlord or listing without authorisation is grounds for immediate account termination.
ℹ️ Verified agents receive a trust badge visible on all their listings — a direct signal of credibility to prospective tenants.
02

Service Fee Policy — No Viewing Fees

🚫 Viewing fees are strictly and unconditionally prohibited. Any agent found charging a viewing fee will face immediate suspension.
  • A service (success) fee is only payable once the tenant has made a confirmed decision to occupy the property.
  • No payment of any kind may be requested before the tenant has reached a decision.
  • Your service fee must be clearly declared upfront and agreed upon once — no surprise additions.
  • No charges beyond the agreed fee are permitted under any circumstance.
  • Any additional appreciation from a tenant must be entirely voluntary and must never be solicited by the agent.
03

Accurate & Honest Property Listings

To reduce unnecessary site visits and protect your time as a professional, listings must be thorough, accurate, and current. Tenants deserve to know exactly what they are viewing before travelling.

📸
Photo Requirements

Upload recent, clear photos of every room, kitchen, bathroom, outdoor spaces, and the building exterior.

🔍
Disclose Faults

Clearly photograph and describe any damages, defects, or issues. Hiding faults is a policy violation.

📋
Accurate Details

Rent, size, location, and amenities must all match reality. Inflated or misleading descriptions are prohibited.

🕐
Keep Listings Current

Remove or update listings immediately once a property is no longer available to prevent wasted viewings.

⚠️ Fake, misleading, digitally altered, or stock photos are strictly prohibited and will result in immediate listing removal and a formal strike against your account.
04

Professional Conduct & Payment Ethics

  • Agents must conduct themselves professionally, respectfully, and honestly at all times.
  • Pressure tactics, misrepresentation of property details, or fee manipulation are strictly prohibited.
  • You represent both LandNant and the property owner — act accordingly.
  • Communications with tenants must remain civil, timely, and transparent.
💡 Your reputation is your business. How you treat your first client determines your next one. Agents with strong ethics consistently earn better ratings and more referrals.
05

Ratings & Client Feedback

LandNant operates a transparent rating system to keep standards high and reward agents who go above and beyond.

  • Tenants may rate agents on honesty, transparency, accuracy of listings, and overall experience after each interaction.
  • Low ratings and repeated complaints directly affect your search visibility and trust status on the platform.
  • Agents who consistently perform well unlock priority placement in search results.
  • Attempting to manipulate, purchase, or coerce ratings is a violation and will result in suspension.
06

Reporting & Enforcement

  • Any tenant or landlord may report a suspected policy violation at any time via the in-app report function.
  • Upon receiving a report, all your active listings will be hidden immediately pending review.
  • You will be contacted within 24 hours to provide your response or clarification.
  • If no response is received within 3 business days, further disciplinary action will be taken.
🚫 Failure to respond, repeated violations, or confirmed fraudulent activity will result in permanent account deletion and removal of all listings with no right of appeal.
07

Agent Discussion Forum

LandNant provides an exclusive discussion space for verified agents to collaborate, share knowledge, and improve the profession collectively.

  • Use the forum to discuss market trends, fee benchmarks, best practices, and platform policy updates.
  • Sharing knowledge that helps other agents operate more professionally is encouraged.
  • Promoting hidden fees, coordinating scams, encouraging policy violations, or sharing false information is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate removal from the forum and the platform.
08

Final Agreement

By registering as an agent on LandNant, you confirm that you are a genuine, authorised representative, that you have read and understood these terms in full, and that you commit to operating with transparency, fairness, and professionalism at all times.

These terms may be updated periodically. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of any revisions.
