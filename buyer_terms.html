1. Using Zedos

  • Zedos provides a platform for buyers to discover and purchase directly from sellers.
  • Zedos does not own or directly sell items listed on the app; purchases are direct agreements between buyer and seller.

2. Account and Eligibility

  • Buyers must be at least 18 years old.
  • Buyers must provide accurate contact and delivery information.
  • Keep login credentials secure and be responsible for activity on your account.

3. Product Listings and Availability

  • Product information is supplied by sellers and Zedos does not guarantee availability or complete accuracy.
  • Review product details carefully before placing an order.

4. Payments

  • Payments are currently made directly between buyer and seller (e.g., cash on delivery, mobile money, bank transfer).
  • Zedos does not process or hold payments at this time.
  • In-app payments may be introduced in the future.
  • Buyers should keep proof of all transactions and pay only to verified sellers.

5. Orders and Delivery

  • Sellers confirm, prepare and deliver orders. Delivery terms are set by the seller.
  • Zedos is not responsible for delivery delays or damages in transit.
  • Orders unanswered by the seller for more than 7 days may be automatically closed.

6. Cancellations and Refunds

  • Cancellations and refunds are handled between buyer and seller.
  • Zedos does not guarantee refunds but may assist in unresolved disputes.

7. Disputes and Complaints

  • Buyers should first contact the seller to resolve disputes.
  • If unresolved, Zedos may review the issue and take action where appropriate.

8. Buyer Conduct

  • Communicate respectfully and avoid false claims or harassment.
  • Do not attempt to defraud sellers or misuse the rating system.

9. Safety and Fraud Prevention

  • Verify seller profiles and product details before payment.
  • Report suspicious listings or behavior to support@zedosapp.com.

10. Zedos’ Role and Limitation of Liability

  • Zedos is a neutral platform and not a party to transactions. Purchases are at the buyer's own risk.
  • Zedos is not liable for product quality, delivery performance, or indirect losses.

11. Account Suspension or Termination

  • Zedos may suspend or close buyer accounts for fraud, policy violations, or misuse.

12. Compliance with Zambian Law

  • Buyers must comply with applicable laws including consumer protection and electronic transactions acts.

13. Updates to These Terms

  • Terms may be amended; significant changes will be communicated via the app or email.
