Zedos — Privacy & Cookie Policy

Last Updated: 16/11/2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Zedos. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy & Cookie Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the Zedos mobile app or related services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone number, profile details, and login credentials.
  • Store Information (for sellers): Store name, logo, product listings, analytics, and activity logs.
  • Order Information: Items purchased, delivery preferences, communication with sellers.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, actions taken, and app interactions for analytics.
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and unique identifiers such as deviceId.
  • Location Data: Only if you enable location permission for relevant features.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your Zedos account.
  • To enable buying and selling of products.
  • To improve app performance, features, and user experience.
  • To send notifications related to orders, promotions, and updates.
  • To detect fraud, abandoned stores, and suspicious activities.
  • To comply with legal obligations when required.

4. How We Protect Your Information

Zedos uses encryption, secure database storage, and access control to keep your data safe. Only authorized personnel and system processes access sensitive information.

5. How Long We Keep Your Information

  • Active accounts: Data is retained as long as your account remains active.
  • Inactive accounts: If no login is detected for 6 months, your account may be automatically closed.
  • Order and transaction records: Retained for audit, safety, and compliance purposes.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your data. We only share information in the following situations:

  • With sellers—when you place an order.
  • With buyers—when viewing your products or store profile (for sellers).
  • With service providers—such as analytics, authentication, storage, and messaging tools.
  • When required by law enforcement or legal processes.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Zedos uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your login sessions.
  • Improve app performance and loading speed.
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience.
  • Support app-based deferred deep linking and referral tracking.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic functionality.
  • Performance Cookies: Helps us understand how users interact with the app.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used to measure traffic, activity, and feature usage.

8. Your Rights

  • Right to access your stored data.
  • Right to update or correct information.
  • Right to delete your account permanently.
  • Right to opt-out of non-essential notifications.

9. Children’s Privacy

Zedos is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy & Cookie Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date and notify users if major changes are made.
